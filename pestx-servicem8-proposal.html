This proposal outlines the integration of your ElevenLabs voice agent with ServiceM8, automating the creation of job tickets from inbound calls. Every call will instantly become a job record—eliminating manual data entry, reducing missed leads, and ensuring consistent customer data.

Scope of Work

1

Connect Voice Agent Webhook

Integrate ElevenLabs post-call webhook with Make.com automation pipeline.

2

Phone Number Normalisation

Standardise caller phone numbers (E.164 format) to local Australian format for accurate ServiceM8 matching.

3

Search Existing Clients

Query ServiceM8 by phone number to identify whether the caller is an existing or new customer.

4

Create Customer or Link Existing

If new: create contact record. If existing: link job ticket to that contact.

5

Populate Job Ticket

Auto-fill ticket with caller name, phone, pest type/issue, call transcript summary, and call date/time.

Why This Matters

Zero Manual Data Entry

Every inbound call automatically becomes a ServiceM8 job ticket. No re-typing. No delays.

No Missed After-Hours Leads

Calls received outside business hours are instantly logged as jobs and assigned, ready for your team the next morning.

Smart Customer Matching

Repeat customers are automatically linked to their existing record; new customers get a fresh profile created.

Full Call Context

Job tickets include the call transcript summary and notes, so your team understands exactly what the customer said.
