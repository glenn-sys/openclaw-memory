## Key Strategic Findings

### Most Important Discoveries

1. **The market gap is real and time-limited**: No NZ/AU agency is offering a productised AI + automation stack for small trades at $400-1,000/month. This window is probably 12-24 months before copycats emerge.

2. **Review automation is the highest-ROI first move**: Post-job automated review requests drive Google Maps ranking, build trust, and are immediately visible to clients. Every client should have this within Week 1.

3. **Tradify NZ Partner Program = fastest path to 10 clients**: $250/referral + access to exactly the ICP. Glenn should apply immediately. Also Jobber affiliate (up to 60% commission on first month).

4. **The Demo closes deals**: Set up a live demo business (callable ElevenLabs agent) this week. "Let me call my demo business right now" converts better than any brochure.

5. **Productise or burn out**: Documented SOPs + a VA by Month 4-5 is the only path to 20+ clients without Glenn being the bottleneck.

6. **Digital moat framing beats "AI receptionist" framing**: Don't sell technology — sell an unbeatable local market position. This justifies higher pricing, prevents churn, and differentiates from every competitor.

### Revenue Projection
- Month 3 (5 clients): ~$2,500 MRR
- Month 6 (10 clients): ~$6,000 MRR  
- Month 12 (20 clients): ~$15,000-17,000 MRR
