8-Week Progression Model

Weeks 1–3
Build
Learn the KB movements. Establish baselines. TGU and snatch take technique — don't rush load.
Week 4
Deload
Drop to 60% load, higher reps. KB flow/movement work only. No PRs.
Weeks 5–8
Load
Progress KB weight each week. Step up to next bell when form is solid for all sets.
Week 8
Test
PR attempts: deadlift, pull-ups, KB snatch reps/weight.

Key Principles — 46yo Edition

  • Recovery > Volume — 8hrs sleep, ~170g protein/day. This is where gains happen.
  • KB technique first — Turkish get-up and snatch reward patience. Ugly reps with heavy weight are wasted reps.
  • Warm up properly — 10 mins minimum. TGU at light weight counts as warm-up on Day 4.
  • Don't train to failure — 1–2 reps in the tank on main lifts.
  • Protect your joints — Fish oil, face pulls, regular mobility. Your shoulders are assets.
  • Deload every 4th week — Mandatory. Your CNS needs it.
  • Zone 2 on off days — Maintain your aerobic base, don't demolish it.
  • KB swings are medicine — Hip hinge pattern, posterior chain, conditioning. Do them every week.
